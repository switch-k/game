ABOUT US

Global institute for management development and training is registered with the corporate affairs commission in Nigeria in the year 2010 and is approved by The Federal Ministry of Education Nigeria in the year 2016. We have a mandate to aid the growth and sustainable development of individuals and corporate institutions across Africa through providing innovative human capital development. Global institute for management development and training offer  world-class professional program in general management which include Digital Marketing, Business Management, Entrepreneurial Development, Project Management Professional, Health, Safety and Environment Competency, Human Resource Management Professional, Customer Service Relationship Management, Organisational Management Professional, Environmental Impact Assessement and other areas of corporate needs. In order to enhance our training standard and capacity , GIMD has develops and maintains an excellent partnership with national and international organisations. Over the years, GIMD has competently trained hundreds of individuals and personnel in different professional fields who are practically making un-quantifiable impacts in their personal and corporate pursuits.
